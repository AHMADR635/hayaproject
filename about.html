About Us

Who We Are

Established in 2005 and our company providing leading edge technology-driven and customer focused services to the Middle East Oil and Gas Industry in association with its international partners

Our Mission

To provide technology-driven and customer focused value adding services for the oil and gas industry in Middle East

Our Vision

To become one of the leading Indigenous oil and gas and Transportation service companies in Middle East by 2025

Haya Co. is led by a dedicated team of seasoned industry professionals and experienced businessmen committed to providing international service levels at commercially competitive prices locally in Yemen.

We achieve this by working closely with our international partners and developing locally based capacity underpinned by a core team of competent professionals.
